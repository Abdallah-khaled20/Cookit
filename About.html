About Recipe Filter App

Welcome to the Recipe Filter App! This app helps you filter recipes based on your dietary preferences. Whether you're vegan, gluten-free, or just looking for a healthy option, we provide an easy way to find recipes that match your needs.

How It Works

Simply select your dietary preference from the filter options and browse through the available recipe cards. Each recipe contains an image, a brief description, and relevant dietary information.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make it easier for people to find meals that align with their dietary needs while keeping the cooking process fun and straightforward.
